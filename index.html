Our product call

Failed transactions are the single biggest thing hurting TakaPay — and it splits into two very different fixes

"Failed transaction" posts are a third of all brand mentions, more than any other topic, and they run deeply negative. But it's not one problem: people are either watching a transfer sit stuck for hours or days, or getting charged for a mobile recharge that never arrived. Those need different owners and different fixes, so we broke it apart instead of reporting one "failed transaction" number.
